Meet Jo.

Everyone's a magpie for something, an enthusiasm on the verge of obsession. Jo's shiny objects come in two forms: travel and photography.

This website is a cumulation of the way she globe-trots, with a penchant for good food, hikes and architecture. The Italian family that introduced her to cavallo tartare, that late night conversation with travellers by the fireplace, the massifs whose grandeur took her breath away; to travel is to acknowledge that people, place and cuisine are irrevocably intertwined. Jo attempts to capture these experiences through her Fuji's lens, blurry stills attesting to the spontaneity and authenticity of the moment.

A music nerd, Jo's talents lie not so much in composition but in curating playlists - one for every season, mood or reason to celebrate. Her love for learning new things makes her a constant flurry of activity, with coding and working the Singer machine as her latest projects.

Come indulge in her soft frantic company.
